About.

The Past

I was born in Port Elizabeth and then grew up in Johannesburg. I was already fascinated by what computers could do and when I moved to Cape Town, I was introduced to computers as a subject at school and my curiosity grew.

I tried to enroll at university after matric with no luck. I worked for some companies but this dream of mine kept ringing. In 2019, I decided to try again and finally, I got admitted, I completed my first year but dropped out. I couldn't afford tuition.

The Present

Since then, I decided to learn on my own. I created projects that aligned with what I wanted. I work for Capitec Bank. They invest in their employee's potential to grow and chase their dreams. Currently I am learning Data Engineering through Umuzi.

My expertise lies in web development with HTML, CSS, and basic JS. I also have experience with PHP and MySQL for database work. At Umuzi, I've learned Python, GIT, command line programming, and clean coding practices.

The Future

I envision a future driven by AI. I am committed to harnessing this technology to empower people to lead better lives. I am dedicated to acquiring knowledge to become a Full Stack Developer and establishing businesses within I.T.

I aim to stay fully informed about the latest tech trends, such as robotics, Large Language Models, Automation, Data storage and manipulation. I'm enthusiastic about becoming an active member of the community of tech innovators.
